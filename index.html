What is the capital asset pricing model?
The capital asset pricing mode is a financial model that calculates the expected rate of return for an investment.
How to calculate capital asset pricing model?
To calculate capital asset pricing model you should use the following formula:
risk premium of the asset = beta * (broad market return - risk free interest rate)
